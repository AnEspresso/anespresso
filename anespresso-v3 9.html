The big picture before you dive in
What Is AnEspresso?
☕
AnEspresso is a real-time break coverage board for your anesthesia team. It replaces the physical whiteboard so that everyone can see the same board on their phone, updated live as changes happen.
Two Roles
📋
Board Runner — Manages the board for the day. The runner marks breaks as complete, activates or deactivates clinical sites, and monitors who needs bathroom coverage.
👩‍⚕️
CRNA — Selects a room assignment and communicates break preferences to the runner. CRNAs can indicate whether they would like their break earlier, later, or not at all. They can also flag urgent bathroom breaks, which appear instantly on the runner's board.
Real-Time Sync
🔄
Every change syncs to all connected devices within seconds. When the runner marks a break, sets a note, or activates a site, every phone reflects the update automatically. No manual refreshing is needed.
Three Break Windows
🕐
The board is divided into three tabs: Morning, Lunch, and Afternoon. Each window tracks breaks independently. The app automatically selects the correct window based on the time of day, but you can switch between them manually at any time.
Daily Reset
🌅
The board resets each morning. All break states clear, CRNA preferences reset, and clinical sites return to their defaults. On weekdays, all sites start active. On weekends, all sites start deactivated, and the runner selects which sites are running that day.
No App Download Required
📱
Simply open the link in any mobile browser and you are ready to go. You can also add it to your Home Screen for a native app experience, complete with the AnEspresso coffee cup icon.

Board Runner Role
Manages the break board for the day
Getting Started
1
Open the link and tap Board Runner to log in. The app will automatically select the current break window based on the time of day.
2
On weekdays, all clinical sites start active and ready to go. On weekends, a setup screen will appear so you can select which rooms are running that day.
Marking Breaks
3
Tap any room to mark its break as done ✓. If you need to undo it, tap the room again and confirm.
4
Use the Morning, Lunch, and Afternoon tabs to manage each break window. Each window tracks breaks independently.
Editing Sites
5
Tap Edit on any category, then choose Activate / Deactivate to toggle individual rooms on or off for the day.
6
Use Deactivate All or Reactivate All to toggle an entire category at once.
7
To add a temporary room, tap Edit then Add Site.
Other Tools
✏️
The notes field under each category allows you to leave messages visible to all users.
🚨
The Urgent banner displays active bathroom break requests. Tap to mark complete.
📋
Tap Feedback next to Log Out to view bug reports and feature requests from staff.
👩‍⚕️
CRNA Role
View the board and set your preferences
Getting Started
1
Open the link and tap CRNA. Choose Room Assignment or Breaker.
Break Preferences
2
For each break window, let the runner know your preference:
Earlier — You would prefer an early break.
Later — You would prefer a later break.
Got Self — You arranged your own coverage.
Bathroom Breaks
3
Tap 🚨 Bathroom Break to send an immediate alert to the runner's board.
Viewing the Board
4
Scroll down to see the full break board. Your assigned room is highlighted with a gold outline.
